Welcome to Clinical Insights AI Assistant

Clinical Insights AI helps healthcare providers generate quick, AI-powered assistants who creates insights from patient visit notes. It offers tools to generate summaries, CPT and ICD-10 codes, medication adherence prompts, and patient-friendly lab result summaries.

Features

    Summarize patient visit notes.
    Generates appropriate CPT codes for medical billing.
    Recommends ICD-10 diagnosis codes.
    Creates patient-friendly emails summarizing lab results with trends.
    Facilitates healthcare providers by delivering quick, concise insights into patient histories.
    Provides medication adherence reminders based on patient medication responses.
    Curates follow-up needs mentioned in past visit notes, summarizing them for easy physician reference.

Prompter Types

CPT Code Prompter: Extracts appropriate CPT codes for medical billing.

ICD-10 Code Prompter: Recommends ICD-10 codes based on patient visits, including HCC and SDOH codes.

Chart Summary Prompter: Provides a concise summary of the patient visit for healthcare providers.

Lab Result Email Prompter: Generates patient-friendly emails summarizing lab results.

Medication Adherence Prompter: Provides simple, patient-friendly medication adherence messages to encourage proper use of prescriptions.

Follow-Up Prompter: Curates a list of follow-ups mentioned in past visits, providing physicians with a quick overview of appointments or tests the patient needs, along with the visit date and service needed.

This project provides AI-powered insights into patient visit notes, generating medical summaries, CPT and ICD codes, and lab result summaries, while also maintaining HIPAA-compliant patient communication.
